B.Sc. Civil Engineering Department

The B.Sc Civil Engineering programme at UCP provides a learning environment for people who have an interest in natural and man-made environments. The programme focuses on technical knowledge of design, construction, implementation and maintenance of several kinds of infrastructures. With studying real-world design problems, students are trained to develop feasible solutions. The faculty focuses on ensuring that students make the best decisions for their academic programme and future career success.

B.Sc. Electrical Engineering Department

The B.Sc Electrical Engineering programme focuses on providing a good understanding of development, maintenance and management in the field of robotics, control systems, telecommunication and building services. Students are taught to analyse, test and modify processes based on findings, providing them with hands-on experience using highly updated technology and equipment. Our students can become future technologists and enter the engineering workforce. They can contribute positively to the public and private sector. With highly experienced faculty, we guide our students to face every challenge in the field of electrical engineering.

B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering Department

The B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering is a programme that is best fit for people whose strengths lie in math and science with interests in materials, robotics, design, high-performance machinery, manufacturing and mechanical operations. The B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering cultivates learning and problem-solving abilities needed to modify, develop and exercise responsible leadership through times of swift change. Providing hands-on experience and co-operative training prepares the students to develop career opportunities as engineers.

BS Computer Science Department

The BSCS programme offers a wide range of courses helping the students in acquiring expertise in computer applications, modern theoretical aspects of computer science, programming techniques and efficient utilization of technology. Students will participate in seminars, workshops as well as competitions to ensure that they keep up the latest trends in the field. The programme ensures highly skilled graduates who are equipped with the required knowledge to lead a successful career in the field of computer science and contribute to the technical community.

BS Software Engineering Department

The Faculty of Information Technology (FoIT) has proactively been playing its role in producing knowledgeable and proficient computer science students. Software development has increasingly been gaining importance in our daily life activities: education, health, communications, manufacturing, banking and finance, transportation, journalism, infotainment, agriculture, and smart communities. Consequently, the software development market has grown rapidly in size and application, and now amounts to billions of dollars annually pouring into the industry worldwide.
